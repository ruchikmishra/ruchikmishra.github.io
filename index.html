I am a second year PhD student at the University of Louisville pursuing my research in the overlapping areas of Robotics, Affective Computing and Healthcare. My current research is on robotic intervention of children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). This involves the use of Deep Learning to understand `engagement' during a therapy session.
