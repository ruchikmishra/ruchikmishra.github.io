Introduction

Hi, I am a PhD graduate from the University of Louisville. My research lies in the overlapping areas of robotics, affective computing, deep learning and healthcare. I am passionate about developing diagnostic tools and interventions using AI for healthcare. In the past, I have worked with affective robotics to develop affect aware robotic interventions for children with Autism Spectrum Disorder. For the forseeable future, I plan to continue down this path of developing AI for translational healthcare.

Open to Work: I am currently on the job market (academic/industry) starting June 2025. If you believe I am a good fit, please feel free to contact me.
