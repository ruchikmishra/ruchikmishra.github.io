Introduction

Hi, I am a third year PhD cadidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Louisville, KY. My current research spans the areas of Affective Computing, Deep Learning and Robotics and their application in healthcare. I am working towards making a robotic intervention of children with Autism Spectrum Disorder. Since datasets for neurodivergent population is scarce, my focus is on conducting human subjects studies to understand multimodal data to make predictions of the engagement levels of individuals during the intervention. This will help make robotic systems more sphisticated towards personalizing interventions.
