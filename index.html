Introduction

Hi, I am a second year PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Louisville, KY. My current research spans the areas of Affective Computing, Deep Learning and Robotics and their application in healthcare. It involves modeling engagement during a therapy session of children with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). My prime focus is to leverage the deep learning algorithms to create an emotion feedback between a robot and a child in the context of autism therapy to facilitate personalized and adaptive robotic interventions.
